Expected list of features in the future.
Things in BOLD are already implemented.

  • Add recipes
  • Delete/remove recipes (only the owner)
  • Warning about duplicates when adding recipe
  • Searching for possible duplicate recipes
  • Advanced search (on the more complex columns, such as the description, instructions and ingredients)
  • Edit recipes (only the owner)
  • List and search for recipes based on column data
  • Make recipes private, only available for owner unless “published”
  • List of last X recipes used
  • Categorize/tag recipes
  • Add/remove a picture to recipe (the main picture)
  • Add/remove pictures to the instructions-field in recipes
  • System for marking/tracking when you last used/cooked recipe
  • Add/remove comments to recipe
  • Edit existing comments
  • Reply to specific comments in recipe
  • Users (login, session and authentication system)
  • Make copy of existing recipes
  • Add/remove recipe to/from favorite list
  • Undo-function when editing recipes (memento pattern)
  • Localization, ability to switch languages (DK, NL and EN) depending on how many users we end up with

NOTE: This is not a exhaustive list, nor is it guaranteed that all things on this list will be implemented. These are things I would like to implement at some point. They are also in random order, and does not reflect priority or anything.

ABOUT RESPONSIVE DESIGN:

Remembering the struggles I had with the wedding website I have decided already NOT to make this thing mobile friendly. At best it'll work for tablet-sized screens and up. Being just one guy and someone who is less interested in layout and design than I am in the backend this just isn't gonna happen. Also: designing for mobiles means designing for tall and narrow, which in my mind really restricts what you can do at certain points.

I primarily develop (and therefore test) this program against Chrome. It should work for Firefox, IE (11), Microsoft Edge and Safari but I have not focus tested it on those browsers. Let me know if something comes up that appears to break, either functionally or in terms of layout.

LAYOUT AND DESIGN:

I'm not 100% happy with the layout/design yet, so what you see may not be final. I am also not happy with how navigation feels (mostly from a backend perspective). That means I ended up hacking a few things towards the end because I aim at going back at re-designing it later on once I figure out a good way of doing it.

OKAY, BUT WHAT'S NEXT?

My plan for the next feature implementation are pictures, for the recipe and in the recipe instructions. I have little to no experience with file uploads and images so I need to do some proper research first. For now I am mostly interested in user experience feedback and nailing the core experience of adding and editing recipes.
